Tajweed Rules for Beginners

Tajweed means reciting the Holy Quran correctly by giving every Arabic letter its proper pronunciation and rights. Learning Tajweed helps Muslims read the Quran beautifully and accurately.

Why is Tajweed Important?

The Holy Quran should be recited as it was revealed. Tajweed helps avoid pronunciation mistakes and improves fluency.

Basic Tajweed Rules

  • Correct Makharij (letter pronunciation)
  • Rules of Noon Saakin
  • Rules of Meem Saakin
  • Ghunnah
  • Madd (elongation)
