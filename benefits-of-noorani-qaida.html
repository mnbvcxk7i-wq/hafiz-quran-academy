Benefits of Noorani Qaida

Noorani Qaida is the first and most important step for anyone beginning to read the Holy Quran. It teaches Arabic letters, pronunciation, and the basic rules needed for correct Quran reading.

Why is Noorani Qaida Important?

Students learn the correct pronunciation of every Arabic letter before reading the Quran. This helps avoid mistakes and builds a strong foundation.

Main Benefits

  • Easy for beginners
  • Improves pronunciation
  • Builds confidence
  • Prepares students for Quran reading
  • Suitable for kids and adults
